Additional Phase 2 Opportunities
More Ways AI Can Work for You
📋
AI File Preflight Checker
Catch bad files before they hit production

Right now, when a customer uploads a file that's low resolution, wrong size, or the wrong color mode — your staff catch it after the fact, email the client, and wait for a new file. Jobs get delayed. Time gets wasted.

An AI preflight check runs the moment a file is uploaded through InfoFlow. It checks resolution, bleed settings, color mode, and file format — and immediately tells the customer exactly what needs to be fixed before the job ever enters your queue.

Result: Fewer back-and-forth emails, fewer production delays, faster turnaround for the customer.
🔔
Automated Order Status Notifications
Stop answering "is my order ready?" calls

Customers call or email to ask if their order is done. Your staff stop what they're doing, look it up, and respond. This happens dozens of times a week across the team.

When a job moves to "complete" in InfoFlow, an automated notification fires — SMS, email, or both — letting the customer know their order is ready for pickup or on its way. They get notified before they even think to ask.

Result: Fewer inbound calls, better customer experience, staff focus on actual work.
📊
Upsell & Cross-Sell Intelligence
Turn InfoFlow's order history into a revenue tool

InfoFlow has every order every customer has ever placed. Right now that data just sits there. A business that orders business cards but has never ordered envelopes, letterhead, or branded folders is a warm upsell waiting to happen — and nobody's reaching out.

AI analyzes your order history, identifies the gaps, and triggers targeted outreach automatically — "Hey, you ordered postcards last spring — want to add door hangers for your next campaign?"

Result: More revenue from existing customers with zero cold outreach effort.
📮
AI-Powered Direct Mail Personalization
Make your mailing services more valuable to every client

You already offer mailing services. Most clients send the same message to everyone on their list. Variable data printing — where each piece is personalized to the recipient — dramatically outperforms generic mail, and most small businesses don't know how to do it.

AI can segment a client's mailing list, generate personalized copy for each segment, and feed it directly into your variable data print workflow — turning a commodity service into a premium offering.

Result: Higher value per mailing job, stronger client results, harder to replace as a vendor.
