Tariff & Freight Handling
Business rules for extraction, mapping, and reporting
ℹ️
Key Rule
Tariffs and freight passed through to customers at no markup as separate invoice line items. They use dedicated Vision part numbers and separate GL accounts. Never merge tariff and freight into one part number.
🏷️ Tariff Rules
Part Number: KSCTARF ⏳ Confirm
GL Account: Separate from freight
Report: Sum all lines per job — no line-by-line breakdown needed
Customer invoice: One line, no markup
Phase 1 exclusion: Vendor with tariff on every line is excluded
🚚 Freight Rules
Part Number: Different from tariff ⏳ Confirm
GL Account: Different GL from tariff
Treatment: On job, passed through at no markup
⚠️ Critical: Freight ≠ Tariff. Never merge.
OPEN Confirm exact tariff part number
OPEN Confirm freight part number

🔄 HOW OAUTH2 TOKENS WORK IN n8n (No Manual Steps After Initial Setup)
Once you add the Microsoft 365 OAuth2 credential in n8n with the Tenant ID, Client ID, and Client Secret from step 3, n8n handles everything automatically:
POST https://login.microsoftonline.com/{tenantId}/oauth2/v2.0/token
It requests a token, stores it, attaches it as a Bearer header on every API call, and refreshes it automatically when it expires. You never touch a token manually.
